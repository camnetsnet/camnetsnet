CNDay 2013 will take place on Tuesday, 7th May 2013, at the Sainsbury Laboratory. Thanks to the generous sponsorship of the University of Cambridge and the Sainsbury Laboratory, the event is free of charge, though registration is required.  

Confirmed speakers include:  
- Albert-Lászlo Barabási, Northeastern University, Boston  
- Marc Barthélémy, CEA Institut de Physique Théorique, Saclay, France  
- Ed Bullmore, Department of Psychiatry, University of Cambridge  
- Ginestra Bianconi, Queen Mary University of London  
- Jörg Menche, Northeastern University, Boston  
- Cecilia Mascolo, Computing Laboratory, University of Cambridge  
- Felix Reed-Tsochas, Saïd Business School, Oxford  

The event also features a poster session. Abstract submissions for posters are accepted until 2nd April 2013. For submissions or inquiries, email cnninfo@hermes.cam.ac.uk.
