# About the event 

The Cambridge Networks Network launch event brought together nearly 90 academics from 23 different departments and institutes across Cambridge. This initial meeting featured short talks by leading researchers across a range of fields, including systems biology, brain mapping, economics, computer
networks, and epidemiology. 

Speakers included Dr Madan Babu (Systems Biology Group, MRC
Laboratory of Molecular Biology), Professor Ed Bullmore (Brain Mapping Unit, Department
of Psychiatry), Professor Sanjeev Goyal (Faculty of Economics), Professor Cecilia Mascolo
(Networks and Operating Systems Group, Computer Laboratory), and Dr Franco M Neri
(Epidemiology and Modelling Group, Department of Plant Sciences).
